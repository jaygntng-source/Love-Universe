# ✨ OUR LITTLE UNIVERSE

### A Digital Love Story Between Fikri & Zahra

---

## 📖 About

Premium interactive romantic website showcasing the journey of Fikri & Zahra — a relationship that started from a simple Mobile Legends encounter.

---

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Clone or download** this repository
2. **Add your photos** to `assets/images/memories/` (10 photos, 4:3 ratio)
3. **Add food photos** to `assets/images/food/` (6 photos, 4:3 ratio)
4. **Add your song** to `assets/music/song.mp3`
5. **Open `index.html`** in your browser
